Activities

History...
During your stay, enjoy the visit of gorgeous sites: like the 'Castello di Loretello' from the 16th century (just 5 minutes away from the house), experience the busy markets, fine leather producers, skilled paper craftsmen in Fabriano, or visit one of the beautiful symphonic concerts in the famous Pergolesi Theatre in Jesi.

Discoveries...
During your stay, enjoy all around sports activities including swimming and diving at one of the most beautifull beaches of italy in Conero, biking (bikes are available of no charge), karting, kayaking, or hiking in one of the biggest caves of Europe la 'grotta di Frassassi'.
