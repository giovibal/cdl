The House

In a peaceful landscape, surrounded by vineyard and olive groves, between rosemary and lavender… A small library…for a moment of rest... small places to relax… and little details of charm... Feel like home in this comfortable house... with tasteful furniture, cheminée, a beautiful open Kitchen...

The private house has the capacity for 2-4 persons max. in order to enjoy a peaceful stay. The house is apprx. 75 m2. Additional services, like fine soap making, authentic italian cooking, and wine tasting at the friendly local winegrower, are available upon request.
